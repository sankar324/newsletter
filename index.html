UrbanPiper Partnership Team is really happy to announce the POS Health Index, commonly referred to as PHI. PHI is measured to analyze your performance month over month, based on which you will be categorised into tiers. Each tier will have certain rewards entailed with it. A lot of services will be extended exclusively for you, for having made it to that tier.

How is PHI measured?

PHI is measure based on four major parameters:

  1. Onboarding Metrics: Based on the number of units onboarded, number of units that go live, average onboarding time of a merchant, etc.
  2. Support Metric: Based on the quality and the quantity of support tickets raised per merchant.
  3. Tech Stability Metric: Based on the number of downtimes that occur.
  4. Payment Metric: Based on the number of days it takes to clear the pending dues.
